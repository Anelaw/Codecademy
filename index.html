Ankara Style Trends 2019

By Anel

One advantage of customizing your clothes is that you can get as creative as you'd like with the style you decide to make, and you'd have yourself a unique design...albeit temporarily as copycats..like me are sure to recreate it if we love it! Ankara fabric can be both amazingly fun & incredibly overwhelming, especially if you're not used to lots of patterns. Hopefully with the following pictures, you'll become a converted Ankara lover!

Jogger and Bomber Jacket Combo

This style would probably trend for a long while. It's comfortable and fun. From travel, to hang-outs, this outfit would make you stand-out..in a good way!

Maxi Skirts

This lovely piece can be dressed down with a pair of fancy flat sandals or cute white sneakers. You can also mix and match with plain colored tops or jeans and pants

Wedding Outfit

You can go all out and customize your Ankara and be a show stopper at any event
